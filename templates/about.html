How It Works

Upload Your Business Documents

Begin by uploading your business documents using the form on our homepage. Use the checkboxes to select the types of test cases you need.

Automatic Analysis with ChatGPT

Once uploaded, your documents are processed by ChatGPT, to generate a comprehensive set of test cases based on the provided details.

Organized Test Cases in Excel

Generated test cases are structured into a clear table and converted into an Excel spreadsheet, ensuring easy management and thorough evaluation.

Download Your File

Finally, the Excel file is made available for download. You will receive a well-organized document ready for you to use in your testing process.
